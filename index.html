About MAKITA

Makita is a leading Japanese brand in the global power tool industry, known for innovation, durability, and exceptional professional performance. Founded in Japan over a century ago, Makita has built a strong reputation by creating tools that blend advanced technology, ergonomic comfort, and outstanding reliability. Its extensive range includes drills, angle grinders, saws, batteries, and accessories designed for professionals and demanding DIY users. Every Makita tool is engineered for power, precision, and long-lasting efficiency, ensuring superior results on any job site. With a continuous focus on safety, user comfort, and productivity, Makita remains a trusted partner for construction, renovation, woodworking, and industrial applications worldwide.
